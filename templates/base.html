Ecosystem Health Assessment Tool

In Northern Europe, it is estimated that 75 per cent of all wild-blooming plants depend upon insect pollination, and most of the flowers are pollinated by honeybees and bumblebees. All the crops, fruit trees and wild flowers blooming before midsummer are dependent on visits of bees to be able to develop their seeds, berries and fruits. The economic value of bee pollination in nature and the great ecological importance of that cannot be counted, but for sure, it must be much greater than the economic value of crop pollination.
